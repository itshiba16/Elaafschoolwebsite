About us

Learn about our school

Elaaf School System provides quality Education through English medium to the children. The School is managed by Divine Child School, adjoining Assembly hall, Underground laboratory, airy classes and is blessed by Outstanding teachers . The school provides each student an exciting and rigorous educational experience designed to help each of them to grow to become a caring, thoughtful, principled, personally and socially well balanced human being. Each child will have opportunities to become technologically literate, learn to communicate effectively, build teams and take risks in life in supportive environment.

Students engage in self-learning activities, in groups or under supervision as you will note in the exhibit, and are provided multiple resources they can choose from. Students might be assigned to research, read, write a story, plan a project to build robots, Skype to conference or collaborate, use a model, or play a game to relax.

Through Sports, we encourage our students to grow and develop both as people and players. We organize incorporated different games such as sack race, three-legged race, gymnastics, and tug of war which gave students the chance to showcase their team spirit, self-discipline, teamwork, and sportsmanship.

We encourage our students to think outside the box. Creativity is a valuable skill that can be applied across subjects and in various aspects of life. Nurturing creativity from a young age helps students become innovative thinkers.
